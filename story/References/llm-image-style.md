# Stilistische Anweisungen für Bildgenerierung

## Der ungeplante Pfad - Odo's Abenteuer

### Basis-Stil-Prompt (für jede Generierung voranstellen)

```
Classic storybook illustration style, warm and inviting atmosphere,
Tolkien-inspired hobbit aesthetic. Watercolor and soft gouache textures,
hand-painted feeling with visible brushwork. Color palette: warm earth
tones (amber, ochre, moss green, warm brown), accented with cozy golden
light from lanterns and fireplaces. Soft edges, gentle lighting,
nostalgic and timeless quality. Reminiscent of classic children's book
illustrators like Tove Jansson, Ernest Shepard, and Alan Lee's Tolkien
work. No photorealism, no 3D rendering, no anime/manga style.
```

### Negative Prompts (immer verwenden)

```
no modern elements, no technology, no electricity, no plastic,
no contemporary clothing, no humans (only hobbits), no anime style,
no photorealistic, no 3D render, no dark/horror elements, no violence,
no sharp digital edges, no oversaturated colors, no lens flare
```

## Charakterrichtlinien

### Hobbits allgemein

- Kleiner als Menschen (etwa 1m groß)
- Große, behaarte Füße - niemals Schuhe
- Runde, freundliche Gesichter
- Lockiges Haar (braun, honigfarben, rotbraun)
- Gemütliche, leicht rundliche Körper
- Kleidung: Westen, Hemden, Hosenträger, erdige Farben, Flicken erlaubt

### Odo (Protagonist, 12 Jahre)

```
Young hobbit boy, 12 years old, curious intelligent expression.
Curly brown slightly unruly hair. Large green eyes, a bit too big
for his face, giving him an alert, observant look. Average build
for a hobbit child. Often has pockets bulging with collected items.
Wears simple earth-toned clothing - red vest, loose slightly greenish shirt, dark brown short trousers.
Bare feet with curly hair on top. Expression: curious, thoughtful,
sometimes mischievous.
```

### Die Familie Tuk

- **Adelard (Vater)**: Rund, freundlich, ordentlich gekleidet, Verwalter-Ausstrahlung
- **Mirabella (Mutter)**: Dunkle Locken, wissender Blick, lacht viel, Mehlstaub an der Schürze
- **Esmeralda (17)**: Älteste, vernünftig, praktisch, ordentlich
- **Dodo & Dora (14)**: Zwillinge, identisch, verschmitzt, immer zusammen
- **Largo (10)**: Rundlich, immer essend oder ans Essen denkend

## Ortsrichtlinien

### Das Auenland (Außen)

```
Rolling green hills with round hobbit doors built into hillsides.
Pastoral English countryside meets fantasy. Patchwork fields,
hedgerows, small bridges over streams, lazy windmills, apple orchards.
Winding dirt paths. Warm sunlight or cozy overcast. Chimneys with
gentle smoke. Round windows glowing warm. No mountains visible,
gentle and safe landscape.
```

### Die Tuckberge (Innen)

```
Vast underground hobbit mansion carved into a great hill. Warm,
cozy despite size. Lit by lanterns, candles, and fireplaces.
Mix of grand halls and intimate tunnels. Round doorways, arched
stone ceilings. Wood paneling, colorful rugs, tapestries. Many doors
of varying sizes and styles. Some areas well-maintained, others
dusty and forgotten. Warm amber lighting, deep comfortable shadows.
```

### Odos Zimmer

```
Small cozy hobbit bedroom at the end of a quiet tunnel. Round window
with view of hillside. Simple wooden bed with patchwork quilt.
Shelf filled with collected treasures: interesting stones, feathers,
a rusty cogwheel, small curiosities. Small writing desk, always
cluttered. Hand-drawn map of the Shire on the wall. Intimate,
personal space reflecting a curious mind.
```

## Stimmungen nach Szenentyp

### Gemütliche Szenen (Familie, Essen, Zuhause)

```
Warm golden lighting, soft shadows, steam rising from food,
crackling fireplace glow, comfortable and safe atmosphere,
rich warm colors, soft focus on backgrounds
```

### Mysteriöse Szenen (Entdeckungen, Geheimnisse)

```
Dramatic lighting with deep shadows, single light source,
cooler color accents against warm base, sense of wonder,
slightly muted palette, focus on details that reveal mystery
```

### Außenszenen (Auenland, Natur)

```
Natural daylight, soft clouds, pastoral tranquility,
rich greens and earth tones, gentle breeze suggested in
grass and leaves, idyllic but not saccharine, lived-in landscape
```

### Herbst-Atmosphäre (aktuelle Jahreszeit der Geschichte)

```
Autumn colors: gold, orange, red, brown leaves. Misty mornings,
rain on windows, harvest vegetables, cozy indoor contrast to
grey outdoor weather. Puddles on paths, bare branches beginning
to show, last apples on trees.
```

## Technische Empfehlungen

### Seitenverhältnisse

- **Landschaften/Panoramen**: 16:9 oder 2:1
- **Innenräume/Szenen**: 4:3 oder 3:2
- **Charakterfokus**: 4:5 oder 3:4 (Portrait)
- **Quadratisch für Icons**: 1:1

### Auflösung

- Mindestens 1024px auf der kürzeren Seite
- Für Druck: 2048px+ empfohlen

### Konsistenz zwischen Bildern

Bei Serien immer hinzufügen:

```
consistent style with previous illustrations, same artistic hand,
matching color palette and lighting approach, cohesive series
```
